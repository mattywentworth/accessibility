Dyslexia Assist

Customize font settings to make text more readable

Pick a dyslexia-friendly or highly readable font, then choose where it should apply. Your choices are saved in this browser and used on every page of this website (Home, About, demos, and this settings page). Different people prefer different typefaces; try a few to see what works for you.

Font Settings

Choose a readable font and which parts of the site should use it.

Lexend, Atkinson Hyperlegible, and Andika load from Google Fonts. Comic Sans uses fonts installed on your device (common on Windows and macOS). OpenDyslexic is hosted with this site.

When enabled, all text on the website will use your selected font, regardless of individual element settings below.
