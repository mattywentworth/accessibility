Making Digital Spaces More Accessible

A collection of feature upgrades that promote accessibility for people with disabilities

Welcome

Digital accessibility is essential for ensuring that everyone, regardless of their abilities, can participate fully in our online world. This website showcases innovative ideas and demonstrations of how we can improve digital experiences through thoughtful design and technology.

Each feature upgrade featured here addresses real barriers that people with disabilities face when using digital platforms, from screen reader compatibility to visual design improvements.
