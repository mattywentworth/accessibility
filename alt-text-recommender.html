Alt Text Recommender

Ensure your images have high-quality alt text for screen reader users

Upload a photo and provide alt text for it. This feature will analyze your alt text to ensure it accurately describes the image content and is helpful for people using screen readers. Alt text is required to submit your post.

Alt text should be a concise, accurate description of the image that conveys the same information a sighted person would get from viewing it. Good alt text helps people with visual impairments understand the content and context of images.
